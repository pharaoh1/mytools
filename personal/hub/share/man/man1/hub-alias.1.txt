hub‐alias(1) ‐‐ Show shell instructions for wrapping git.  ===

## SYNOPSIS

‘hub alias‘ [‐s] [<SHELL>]


## OPTIONS
  * ‐s:
    Output shell script suitable for ‘eval‘.

  * <SHELL>:
    Specify  the  type	of  shell  (default: "$SHELL" environment
variable).
